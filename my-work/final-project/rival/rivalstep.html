Progress of achievements of "The Rival" on QQ Music

On Aug 24th 00:00, 2019, "The Rivals" was officially released on QQ Music, which is one of Chinese mainstream music platforms for selling digital songs/albums. As soon as it was published,"The Rival" achieved a total sales of 2000,000 and was identified as the "Gold Album" on QQ Music. A fan named "black heart" bought 13736 of them. Another fan named "littlegarden" bought 5415, contributing 0.54%.

On Aug 24th 16:55, 2019, "The Rivals" achieved a total sales of 3000,000 rmb and was identified as the "Triple Platinum Album" on QQ Music. A fan named "black heart" bought 13736 of them, and Official Fan Group bought 164168 of them, contributing 10.94%.
