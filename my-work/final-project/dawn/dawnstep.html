Progress of achievements of "Dawn" on QQ Music

On Aug 7th 10:00, 2019, "Dawn" was officially released on QQ Music, which is one of Chinese mainstream music platforms for selling digital songs/albums. As soon as it was published, "Dawn" achieved a total sales of 1000,000 and was identified as the "Platinum Album" on QQ Music. A fan named "niucanbank" bought 10 of them. "Fan Galaxy" Group bought 220168 of them.

On Aug 7th 10:19, 2019, "Dawn" achieved a total sales of 2000,000 rmb and was identified as the "Double Platinum Album" on QQ Music. "Fan Galaxy" Group bought 220168 of them, and a fan named "Wind and Fire Wheels" bought 89317 of them, contributing 13.18%.

On Aug 7th 13:48, 2019, "Dawn" achieved a total sales of 3000,000 rmb and was identified as the "Triple Platinum Album" on QQ Music. "Fan Galaxy" Group bought 220168 of them, and another "Fan Galaxy" Group bought 243263 of them, contributing 24.31%.
